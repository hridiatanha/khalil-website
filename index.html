About Us

Founded in July 2017, Khalil Biryani House has been serving authentic Bengali biryani, curries, and kebabs to New York and beyond. We use halal-certified ingredients, traditional recipes, and the freshest spices to bring you the flavors of Bangladesh and South Asia. Our mission is to share the warmth of home-cooked food and community with every dish.
